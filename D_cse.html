About Department

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering was established in the year 1999.Department of Computer Science & Engineering at the Engineering College Bikaner hasbeen at the forefront of churning out software engineers with a high caliber of technicalexpertise. It continues to foster and engender the innovation and breadth of vision necessaryto excel in the blooming software industry. The department has always attracted the best ofengineering aspirants from all over. The department has a well qualified and experiencedfaculty members. The Department offers B. Tech., M. Tech. And Ph.D. course.
