Civil Department

About Department

One of the primary engineering departments of Govt Engineering College Bikaner, The department of civil engineering offers B.Tech. program. The department was established in 2013. The department has exclusive features such as-

Salient Features of the Department

  • The department consists of a total of 225 students from three batches.

  • A two storey separate building in the college for civil engineering students.

  • Separate tutorial classes and lecture theatres for teaching and problem solving purposes.

  • The department has set its own Material Testing Lab, Structural Engineering, Surveying, and Geotechnical Lab and Building drawing lab.

  • Soon the department is going to set its own Fluid Mechanics, Concrete Technology Lab, Road Material Testing and Geology lab.

  • The department is organizing various Skill Development Activities for students such as Group Discussion, Quiz, and Technical test.

  • Department has a pre planned Academic Schedule for each session.

  • The department organizes various cultural activities such as Celebration of Engineer's Day, Science day.

  • The department has a Cultural Fest named Akriti which is organized every year.

  • Environmental Engineering and conservation studies.

  • Use of Computerized Total Station for offsite Surveying.

  • Mountain and Table Survey for Practical knowledge.
