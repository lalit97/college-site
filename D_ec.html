Electrical&Communication Department

About Department

The Electronics & Communication Engineering department has been established in the year 1999. The department offers B. Tech. (ECE)course with intake of 90 seats. The strength of the department is theexcellent laboratory facilities and highly qualified faculty which guides the students during the program.

Salient Features of the Department

  • Software's like Mat lab for Signal Processing and design.

  • Workshops, Seminars, Paper presentations are arranged to enhance teaching learning skills as well as knowledge upgradation.

  • RF and Optic Design Hardware / Software for design of communication network.

  • HDL software for the design of various circuits in digital electronics.

  • 24 hrs. Internet connectivity.

  • VLSI lab which is in great demand in Electronics Industry.

  • Regular programmes for Personality Development, English Speaking, Yoga, Sports & Cultural activities.

  • Better student-teacher interaction.

  • Dynamic training and placement cell.

  • Microwave lab with Klystron Bench, Gunn Oscillator and Various Passive Components for high frequency comm.

  • Microprocessor lab for Control of Electronic Circuits.

  • Healthy work environment.
